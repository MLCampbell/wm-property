Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I sell my house?

It will take at least 2 weeks for us to buy your property. You should plan for 3-4 weeks unless you specify otherwise. This can have an impact on our offer.

How soon will I receive funds?

Funds will be transferred to a bank account of your choice on the day of completion. This can take longer depending on your bank.

When will I have to move out?

We will work with you to find a date that works for you, funds will hit your account that same day.

What if my house is in poor condition?

Don't worry, we aim to provide an offer for any property. Our offer will reflect the value of your house based on condition, market conditions and the speed that you would like to sell. If we believe we are unable to help you we will let you know as soon as possible.

Do I have to pay for my valuation or any legal fees?

No, all costs incurred to value your property and any legal costs to sell are covered by Zoom Property Buyers.

Will I need a solicitor?

A solicitor is required to conclude the sale of your property but Zoom Property Buyers will provide one free of charge who will work with our solicitor to provide as quick a service as possible. If you wish to use your own solicitor you will be liable for their costs and it could impact on the speed of the process.
